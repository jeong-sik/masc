Note: keyframes.css ships 27 named animations; primitives.css ships an additional 18 anim-* keyframes. Motion tokens compose with both. --motion-exit / --motion-swap / --motion-reveal / --motion-settle currently have no @keyframes pairing in production — they are transition-only or reserved.
