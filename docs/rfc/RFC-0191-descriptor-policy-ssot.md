# RFC-0191 — Withdraw descriptor authorization policy

Descriptors define exact model-visible name/schema and handler projection.
They do not encode audience, maintenance class, approval class, inline safety,
product domain, retry policy, or Keeper visibility. All registered descriptors
are visible; actual external effects use the generic Gate.
