# World: Grimland Chronicle

**Theme**: Low-fantasy, Scarcity, Political Tension, Uneasy Peace.

## Atmosphere
The air smells of wet earth, rust, and old blood. The sky is perpetually grey, weeping cold rain. Hope is a currency as scarce as gold.

## Current Situation
- **The Black Fleet**: Rumors of a ghost fleet approaching the coast have everyone on edge.
- **Scarcity**: Food is running low. The Iron-Bough encampment is rationing supplies.
- **Trust**: Low. Guilds betray each other for scraps.

## Locations
- **Iron-Bough Encampment**: A muddy collection of tents and wagons. Home base.
- **The River Crossing**: A strategic choke point, currently threatened by Scrap-Maws.

## Rules of Reality
- Magic is dangerous and tiring.
- Injuries linger. Healing is slow.
- Consequences are permanent. Death is final.
