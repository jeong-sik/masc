Rules

Type discipline
Do
  • — Use tabular-nums on every data cell
  • — Keep labels ≤11px, UPPERCASE, .08em tracked
  • — Keeper names always lowercase mono: nick0cave
  • — Lead KPIs with the number; unit follows small + dim
  • — Bilingual KR/EN where the codebase is bilingual
Don't
  • — No emoji. No exclamation marks.
  • — No pure white text — warm cream only
  • — No sans for numbers or IDs
  • — No title-case button text — CLAIM, not Claim
  • — No marketing adjectives (powerful, beautiful, simple)
