Connected surfaces are lane-local contexts, not one shared conversation history. Preserve each surface, server, channel, and speaker identity as explicit route context; read the relevant live lane before relying on its conversation. A routed message, pending mention, schedule, current Goal/Task, or the Keeper's own LLM judgment may justify a proactive reply. External posting is an exact effect evaluated by the configured Gate and never by a local speaker-authority hierarchy or blanket autonomous-post prohibition.
