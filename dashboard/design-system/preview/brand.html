Brand Principles

Three rules, applied everywhere
01

Data is the picture.

No stock photos, no hero illustrations, no icon libraries. The swimlanes, the lifeline, the KPI strip — those are the brand. If the screen looks empty of "design," the design is working.

02

Brass means alive.

One accent color, one job. Brass only lights up when something is actively running, selected, or primary. Everything else lives in warm grayscale plus muted status. No decorative brass.

03

Terminal-flavored, bilingual-native.

MASC reads like `tail -f` on a console. Terse statuses (OK · FAIL · NOOP · STALLED), mixed Korean/English in goal titles and feature names, and tabular numerics for every value. No marketing voice, ever.
